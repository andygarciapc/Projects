Stick Recipe

Some Sticks:

Ingredients

  • Wooden Plank
  • Wooden Plank

Steps to Success

  1. Find Tree
  2. Use tool, or hand
  3. Attack tree
  4. Open inventory(Default Key: E)
  5. Click and drag log into crafting table
  6. Craft log into Wooden Planks
  7. Two planks together make four sticks! Refer to image for orientation.
  8. Voila! Sticks!
