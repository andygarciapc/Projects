Stick Recipe

Some Sticks:

Ingredients

  • Wooden Plank
  • Wooden Plank

Steps to Success

  1. Spawn into game
  2. Run around world until you find water
  3. Collect water(Default Interaction Key: E)
  4. To collect the red pigment, you must head all the way north to Shattered Mountain
  5. There is red dye all around the area, good luck!
  6. Magenta Dye is located near BrightWood, same process as before.
  7. Now head to town
  8. Approach a cooking station
  9. Search "Dazzling Coral Dye" in the top left search bar
  10. Hit that button!
  11. Voila! Dye!
